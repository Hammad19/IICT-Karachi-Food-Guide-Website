Top Pizza's of Karachi


There are a lot of types of pizzas that you will find all around the world depending on which region of the world you are living in. Pizzas are prepared with slightly different styles. If you are living in California then most probably you will not have the same pizza experience as someone living in New York because the New York style pizza has thin crust whereas, the California style pizza is much thicker with full of toppings. It is not surprising to hear that pizza is becoming one of the major fast food items in Pakistan. It is one of the highly preferred fast food dish which is liked by almost all age group people. Various different brands of pizza are already in the market but it’s still increasing with more new pizza shops. Every pizza restaurant is trying it’s best to come up with new variations in order to become a brand in this country. The bigger brands are having too many franchises all over the country to make sure it is easily accessible for all. In Karachi, you can easily find top quality pizza shops with exclusive varieties. For pizza lovers, we have compiled a list of all major pizza brands in this city.



1.Pizza Hut


Pizza Hut is an international pizza chain that is having too many branches in Karachi. It was founded by Dan and Frank Carney in the year 1958. It is the biggest pizza chain all over the world with around 16,796 franchises. In Karachi, you can easily find a pizza hut nearly at all the renowned places. The variety of pizza that they offer includes chicken tikka, chicken fajita, afghani tikka, behari, carnivore, bar BQ buzz, tropical hawaian and more.

2.Pizza Crust


Pizza crust is in Block 13-B, Gulshan-e-Iqbal. They have a wide range of pizza including beef, chicken, and vegetable. Some of their specialties include signature crust- stuffed with green chicken, green pepper, onion, jalapeno, green olive, and alfredo. Mix 2- filled with tandoori and green chicken along with mushroom, Italian herb, mix creamy sauce and mozzarella cheese. Mix supreme- packed with smoked chicken, beef, pepperoni, smoked veal, onion, mushroom, capsicum, black olive and mozzarella cheese. Veggie lover- packed with onions, capsicum, mushroom, sweet corns, olive, and tomatoes.

California pizza has five branches in different areas of Karachi. They also offer an extensive range of pizza to make sure everyone got the desired flavor.
