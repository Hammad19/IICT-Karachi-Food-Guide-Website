Top Pasta of Karachi


Pasta is a type of food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking.



1- Sizzlerz Cafe & Grill


If you like creamy cheesy pasta with chicken steak on top and oven-fresh baked bread then sizzlers cafe & grill is the place you must check out. They have the best super creamy Alfredo pasta and parmesan chicken penne pasta in affordable prices. Their pasta doesn’t have something unique or has much going in it but it’s definitely delicious. That creamy alfredo sauce with mushrooms is to die for and the panko fried chicken on top is super soft and juicy.

Another great place is the Otg vintage. Their spicy alfredo pasta and arrabbiata pasta are creamy and succulent. Penne pasta topped with mozzarella cheese and spicy chicken will make you order more. That pasta bowl loaded with sauce, pasta and chicken is enough for one person.

3– Fettuccine


By its name it must be obvious fettuccine serves pasta. Some great affordable pasta is being served at fettuccine located in Gulshan- e-Iqbal. Small cosy cafe and piping hot pasta is a perfect combination. Their alfredo pasta and Arabian penne are amazing. The greatest thing is that they also offer you to make your own paste. In this customized pasta you get to choose the sauce, pasta, toppings, meat and spices. Rouge blanc sauce is really good if you like the mild flavour.

Well, you must have heard of Broadway pizza and its flavoursome pizzas but what you also need to try is their pasta. Broadway’s garlic ranch pasta and bbq ranch pasta are the best kinds of pasta with a ranch sauce you must try asap. And, it’s for less than rs.500.

5- Delfrio


Delfrio’s fettuccine alfredo is appetizing. Creamy Alfredo sauce with grilled chicken breast fillet and mushrooms is something you must try it when you are craving for some quality pasta. The place is even cool and classy so, you can definitely hang out, capture gram-worthy pictures and have some pasta over there with your friends or family. This might just sit right at your budget or cost a rs.100 more than other cheap places but that money is all worth the taste.
