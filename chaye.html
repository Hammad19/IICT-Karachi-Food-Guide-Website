Top 5 Chaye Spots in Karachi

  • Lets Go


All Is Chaye


Following the IT trend of developing chai places in Karachi, we get to see the opening of different chai spots at different areas. After all who wouldn’t love to spend their “chai break” at these casual yet family oriented places? The scrumptious taste of those mouth-watering paratha’s and the sweet smell of chai can get you addicted to it. Especially since the winter season is here so these chai spots will remain the center of attraction for people looking to have a decent cup of tea with a place to share a good laugh with your friends and family. Here are our 5 best Chai spots picks:



1.Chai Shaye


Located at Khayaban-e-Bukhari, Defence. Chai Shai is one of the most popular chai spots in Karachi. Their menu consists of many different styles of Chai with their eliachi chai and pizza paratha being in popular demand. It is a classic film themed café in an open environment with their famous graffiti art designed on the wall. The reasonable price range attracts people from all classes.

2.Tea and Tattle


Also located at Khayaban-e-Bukhari, Defence. Tea and tattle has made its name in the “cafe industry” in a short period of time. They are famous for their deliciously made waffles and tea. A must visit for youngsters who are looking to spend quality time with their friends while having satisfying snacks.

3.Fibbi


Fibbi cafe located at different places in Karachi is an old and a very famous chai cafe. Their beautiful packaging for tea and their orange colored logo makes them stand out. They have been serving quality food and tea since a long time, and their Kashmiri tea is famous among people. The whole idea about having a sip of an amazing tea while sharing good moments with your friends and family in a delightful ambiance.
