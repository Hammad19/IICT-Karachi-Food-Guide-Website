Top Biryani's of Karachi


Biryani is one of the most preferred rice dishes in Pakistan. It is an excellent combination of rice and meat along with different spices. Nothing beats the feeling of having a biryani after a busy day. It is usually prepared in the houses because everyone enjoys the taste of this spicy rice, but it is not less than an art to making biryani with perfect spice combination and ideal boiling level of rice that’s why a lot of people like to eat it in different famous biryani centers. Over time this meal has come up with lots of varieties including meat, vegetable, and seafood biryani. Karachi is full of places where you can easily find this tempting food item. This dish is usually served with a special type of raita along with a salad. It is a food that can be enjoyed in all kinds of weather and almost all the significant occasions. If you are on the list of those people who don’t know where to get the lip-smacking biryani in Karachi, then this article is especially for you.



1.Student Biryani


Student biryani is one of the oldest places that serve delicious biryani in this city. They are still one of the first choices of people who love biryani. It was founded by Haji Muhammad Ali in the year 1969. It is now recognized internationally with branches in Canada, Australia, and the United Arab Emirates. They also have different branches in Karachi where you can get the same variety of this appetizing dish. The variations that student biryani offer include, chicken tikka biryani, beef, and chicken biryani.

2.The White Biryani


The White Biryani is a popular biryani place in Karachi. It has two branches; one is in phase 6 of defense, and the other is in block 5 of Clifton. They offer a pleasant environment and quality standard which makes it an appealing place to enjoy a perfect meal with your friends and family. It is specifically called white biryani because of its white color which is different from the traditional biryani.

3.Biryani Of The Seas


It is one of the fastest-growing biryani places, which is located in Clifton. The critical feature of this biryani is the use of fresh seafood in it. If you are tired of eating traditional style biryani, then it is really a nice place to have some different variations. They also have a massive range of seafood items including Bar B Q Prawns, seafood karahi, seafood rolls, and Chinese seafood.

4.Madni Biryani


Madni biryani is a top quality biryani centre which is in the buffer zone. The chicken biryani that they prepare is of nice flavor with the perfect amount of chicken, and the quality of rice is also good. It is not too much spicy compared to other biryani, that’s why it is ideal for those who want to enjoy mild spice rice at a quality place.

5.Mirchi 360


Mirchi 360 is in phase 5 of defence. It is basically a fast food restaurant with a different variety of items including fish nuggets, chicken makhni, brain masala, afghani kebabs and more. In the rice dishes, the Sindhi biryani that they offer is really exceptional. It is specially made with all the key ingredients that are normally used in the Sindhi biryani to make sure that you enjoy the real taste of it.
