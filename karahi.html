All about Karahi


Nothing is as mouth watering as our desi dishes! No matter how modern we may become, our connection with desi dishes will never weaken. When we talk about desi dishes, Karahi is undoubtedly one of the best and most favourite of all. As much as we love to munch on mum-made karahi at home, eating this dish with ‘garma garam tandoor ka naan’, in a local restaurant tantalizes our taste buds to the max! So, if you are planning to head out this weekend for karahi but don’t have a clue where to go, consider this list as your guide and decide where to go. Well, if you are a real foodie, you can also try all these eateries one by one.



1.Anwar Baloch


Located at Malir Bridge in Malir, Anwar Baloch is one of the most popular karahi eateries in town! They offer a variety of different karahi flavours including chicken karahi, mutton shahi karahi, prawn karahi, chicken brown karahi and batair karahi.

2.Afridi Inn


The local Afridis of Khyber PakhtunKhwa are known as the pioneers of this delicious savoury and at Afridi Inn, their karahi represents the same. Their chefs know how to score a perfect concoction of meat and spices to tantalize those taste buds and make you keep licking your fingers while eating.

3.Shaheen Shinwari


Just like Afridi, the Shinwari tribe too hails from Pakhtunkhwa and shares a historic origin with this exquisite dish. So, if you want to experience that true taste of karahi, head over to the Shaheen Shinwari restaurant and indulge your taste buds in a one-of-a- kind karahi taste.

4.Salateen Restaurant


This is another equally popular go-to spot for karahi lovers in Karachi. In an open air dining area, the menu of Salateen Restaurant offers an array of scrumptious karahi versions; however, the most popular order is their ‘green masala karahi’. Braised chicken with a lot of sprinkled green chillies and coriander, the palatable green masala karahi will make you mop every inch of the gravy from your plate, with your naan.

Tooso offers a range of dishes and cuisines, but their karahi is something that will make you fall in love with this small eatery all over again. The restaurant is situated in Bahadurabad and offers chicken and fish karahi, two of their most popular servings.
