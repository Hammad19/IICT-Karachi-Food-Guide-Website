All about Haleem


Haleem is a delicious amalgamation of wheat, barley, lentil and meat. It is savory in taste and beautifully balanced in flavor.



1.Mazaidar Haleem


Craving for spicy Haleem? Mazaidar Haleem offers you the tastiest Haleem of Karachi at a very reasonable price. If you are a Haleem-lover, you can’t stop yourself from going to this Haleem joint repeatedly! Don’t forget to sprinkle extra “Chaat Masala” on it to double up the taste.

5.Darbar Haleem


For the typical Hyderabadi Haleem, Darbar Haleem is what you should put your hands on. Shower some coriander leaves, ginger, lemon juice and oil on it and ta-da! You have the best Hyderabadi Haleem in town! Reasonable prices with amazing taste, you can’t resist yourselves from going back to these places for Haleem again and again.
